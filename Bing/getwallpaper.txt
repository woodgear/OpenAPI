"src":"https://www.bing.com/az/hprichbg/rb/SachsischeSchweiz_ZH-CN10640382929_1920x1080.jpg",
"width":1920,
"height":1080,
"type":"jpg",
"title":"SachsischeSchweiz",
"introduce":"撒克逊瑞士国家公园",
"location":"德国 ",
"author":"Andreas Vogel" 
}

或者可以直接访问 http://misakimei.com/openapi/bing/wallpaper.json 来获取当天的bing壁纸 json信息
